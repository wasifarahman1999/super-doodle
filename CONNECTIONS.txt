Components:
Arduino, LED, Resistance, IR sensor,Jumper wires(M-M,F-F,M-F)


Connections:
IR sensor:
GND  -GND of Arduino
5V/ VCC  - 5V of Arduino
Outputs(1,2,3,4,5)  - digital pins(2,3,4,5,6)

LED:
Long leg  - digital pin 13 (breadboard-digpin 13)
Short leg  - resistor(330 ohm optional), resistor’s short leg- GND of arduino
